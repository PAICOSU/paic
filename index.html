About Our Lab

Welcome to the Precision Agriculture Innovation Center. Our lab is dedicated to developing cutting-edge technologies to address the challenges in modern agriculture. We focus on integrating sensing, geospatial, automation, and AI technologies to improve crop management, resource efficiency, and sustainability in agricultural systems.


Based in the Department of Crop and Soil Science at Oregon State University, our team works at the intersection of engineering, agronomy, and plant science to develop practical, field-ready solutions for all agricultural stakeholders.
